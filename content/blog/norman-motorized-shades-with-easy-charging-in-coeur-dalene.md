<p>The Norman® <strong>charging wand for motorized shades</strong> is a true game-changer. Imagine a sleek, lightweight wand, about the size of a flashlight. Instead of plugging a cord directly into the shade, you simply touch the end of the wand to a small charging port on the shade motor.</p>
<p>That's it.</p>
<p>The wand automatically connects and begins to recharge your shade's motor. It's a completely cordless process that requires no fumbling or straining. You can stand safely on the floor and recharge your shades with ease.</p>
